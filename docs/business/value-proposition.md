# Value Proposition

## For Individual Professionals

Cold outreach has a low response rate. A message from a mutual trusted contact is dramatically more likely to be read and replied to. Pathorah finds that connection chain automatically — you do not need to manually dig through LinkedIn or your contact book hoping someone knows someone.

## For Business Development Teams

After the April 2026 release, Pathorah supports Company and Branch as pathfinding targets. This means a BD team can now find the shortest warm path to **Vodacom South Africa's Johannesburg office** specifically — not just "someone at Vodacom." This is relevant for enterprise sales motions, fundraising, and partnership discovery.

## Trust Differentiation

Unlike platforms where any connection is treated equally, Pathorah weights edges by warmth (how warm is the relationship?), trust, recency, and seniority. A path through your direct colleague who is also a long-time friend of the target is scored higher than a path through a LinkedIn connection you have never actually spoken to.

The representative verification system extends this trust reasoning to companies: reaching a verified founder IS reaching the company, structurally. An unverified claim provides no such shortcut.
