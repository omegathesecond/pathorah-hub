# Executive Summary

Pathorah is a professional networking platform built on the insight that warm introductions dramatically outperform cold outreach. It finds the shortest verified path between any two professionals — through people and organizations they already know — and facilitates the introduction chain.

## Mission

Make warm introductions accessible and trustworthy at scale, for Africa and beyond.

## Stage

Active development. Core platform is feature-complete for person-to-person introductions. The April 2026 release expanded the graph to include Companies and Branches as first-class targets, significantly expanding the addressable use cases (business development, enterprise sales, fundraising, partnership discovery).
