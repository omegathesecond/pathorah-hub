## Comparable Platforms

| Platform | Approach | Pathorah Difference |
|----------|---------|-------------------|
| LinkedIn | Shows mutual connections; no path scoring | Pathorah finds the specific path and scores it by relationship strength |
| Intro.co | Human-curated introductions | Pathorah is algorithmic and scalable |
| Clay | Contact enrichment | Pathorah focuses on pathfinding, not enrichment |

## Target Segments

- Individual professionals seeking warm introductions for job searches, sales, or networking
- Business development teams targeting enterprise accounts
- Investors seeking warm paths to founders
- Founders seeking investor introductions
